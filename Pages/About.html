About Aviary Beauty Salon

At Glamour Salon, we believe every woman deserves to feel confident and beautiful. Our professional stylists bring years of expertise, creativity, and passion to each client’s look.

Aviary is a women’s beauty salon based in Polokwane, established in 2014 and conveniently located at 55 Landros Mare. Specializing in hair, nails, and lashes, Aviary offers a luxurious and welcoming space where clients can enjoy high-quality beauty treatments tailored to their needs. With years of experience and a commitment to excellence, the salon has become a trusted destination for women seeking professional care, style, and confidence.

Vision

To be Polokwane’s leading beauty destination, inspiring confidence and empowering women through exceptional hair, nail, and lash services.

Mission

To provide personalized, high-quality beauty treatments in a warm and professional environment, ensuring every client leaves Aviary feeling beautiful, valued, and confident.
